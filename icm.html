ICM Stuff
This is a collection of images i created using intentional camera movment. i like to think of these as more painterly than photogrpahic as they allow me to work the scene much more to be an index no so much of a place and time but of an experience or memory. i like to think that the inclusion of multiple slow moments at a particular location infuses even more connection to that place in the final image, showing changes, presence, and connections unseen in a single strait photograph.
